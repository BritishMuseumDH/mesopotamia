Enuma Anu Enlil text

The 'Enuma Anu Enlil' series of tablets is a collection of documents interpreting observations of the stars, planets, weather and natural occurrences like earthquakes.

The tablets were created from information and observations over hundreds of years.

They were kept together like reference books so that scribes could use them to interpret the meaning of certain events and how they would affect the king.
