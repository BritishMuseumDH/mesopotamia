Shumma Alu tablet

The highlighted part of this tablet reads:

1. If the outside of a house looks showy it will be deserted.

2. If a house looks calm, the house will be lucky.

3. If a house looks attractive, it will not last long.

4. If a house looks unattractive, the people living in it will be happy.

5. If a house looks shaded, the people living in it will be happy.

6. If the look of a house keeps changing so will its owners.

7. If a house looks normal, the owner will grow old.
