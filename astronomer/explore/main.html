The scribes of Enuma Anu Enlil

The scribes of Enuma Anu Enlil were a group of men who performed many different duties relating to astronomy and astrology in the Babylonian court around the 6th century B.C. These scribes used observations of the earth and sky that had been collected over hundreds of years.

Texts refer to this select group of scribes, but we still do not know exactly who they were, what they did, and how they were trained. However, the texts demonstrate how experienced they were in observation and interpretation.
