The charioteer

The charioteer drives his chariot towards the battle. He holds the reins of his horses tightly in his hands. The chariot is pulled by two horses with a spare horse alongside.

The horses are ready for battle. A groom holds their bridles until it is time for the charioteer to join the battle. The horses are wearing necklaces.

The charioteer has many weapons with him. He carries a sword in a sheath attached to his belt and there is a spear in the back of the chariot. Hanging on the side of the chariot are two quivers holding axes and many arrows.

In the background are two vultures. One vulture attacks a wounded enemy soldier.
