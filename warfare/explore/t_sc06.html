The siege

Two Assyrian soldiers work together to make a tunnel at the base of the walls under the main gate tower. They remove the bricks one by one. If enough bricks are removed, the tower will collapse.

Enemy soldiers shoot arrows and throw rocks at the Assyrians. Wounded enemy soldiers fall to the ground from the high walls of the city.

Two more Assyrian soldiers chisel away chunks of the city walls with long crowbars. They will continue until they make a hole big enough for soldiers to crawl through, or make the wall collapse.
