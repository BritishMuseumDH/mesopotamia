Necklace with symbols


This necklace is decorated with many important symbols.

This is the forked lightning symbol. It is the symbol of Adad, the storm god.

This is the horned crown. It is the symbol of Ashur the most important Assyrian god.

This is the cross symbol. It was the symbol for Shamash, the sun god.

This is the crescent symbol. It was the symbol of Sin, the moon god.

This is the planet with rays symbol. It is the planet we call Venus which in Mesopotamia was the symbol for Ishtar, the goddess of love and war.
