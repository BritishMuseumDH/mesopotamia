Taurus mountains

To the north of Mesopotamia are the Taurus mountains, which run from northern Iraq to the Mediterranean Sea and then westward.

Both the rivers Tigris and Euphrates rise at the eastern end of these mountains, where they meet the Pontus mountains of northern Turkey and the Zagros mountains of Iran. The Taurus mountains rise to almost 4000m.

When the winter snow melts in the mountains, the flood waters make the Tigris and Euphrates rise several metres.
