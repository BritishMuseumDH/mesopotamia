The Babylonian map of the world

This Babylonian map includes some of the most important cities and regions of Mesopotamia. It was made around 600 B.C. and comes from the city of Sippar.

It is very different from the kind of maps we are used to looking at as some of the names seem to be in the wrong place. In fact this map is more interested in the mysterious lands believed to exist beyond the sea where gods, heroes, animals and monsters lived.
