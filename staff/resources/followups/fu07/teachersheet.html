Why build tall buildings?


 
Explore the use of buildings. Consider why some buildings are made taller for no obvious reason.
Pictures of modern and ancient tall buildings
Group discussion / individual work

 

Remind the pupils about ziggurats.

  • Can anybody tell me something about a ziggurat?
  • Why were ziggurats built so high?
  • What were they built of?
  • Why would you have been able to see ziggurats a long way off in southern Mesopotamia?
Discuss why some buildings are made tall.
  • Can you think of any modern tall buildings?
  • Why do you think they were built so high?
  • Why do high-rise flats save on space?
  • Why do you think offices are built as sky-scrapers?
  • What other kind of buildings need to be tall?
Ask the pupils to make a list of modern tall buildings. Ask them to think of other ancient tall buildings and what were they used for.
  • How many ancient examples can you come up with?
Pharos/lighthouses
Pyramids
Minarets
Houses in Yemen
Church towers/steeples
Sky-scrapers
High-rise flats
  • What are the advantages and disadvantages of tall buildings?
