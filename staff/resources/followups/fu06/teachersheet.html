Measuring length


Measurements in cubits and fingers


 
To find out about different forms of measuring.
String
Teacher presentation / individual work

 

Discuss different ways of measuring things e.g. imperial, metric.

  • Why is measuring important?
  • If I was to make something from measurements you had given me what would I need to know?
Discuss the Mesopotamian methods of measurement.
  • What would be a problem with a measurement based on a finger length?
Give the pupils some string and have them divide it into fingers and cubits by marking it with a pencil. Ask them to choose a number of objects in the class and measure them, recording the results in a table. Compare the results between pupils on the board.
  • Does everybody agree on the measurement?
  • Why might there be differences?
  • What would have to happen for everybody to agree on the size of something?
  • What would happen if you wanted something made and the sizes were different?
  • What would happen if you were a trader and the measurements were different between cities or countries?
Finish by explaining that until a few hundred years ago many countries had different systems of measurement.
