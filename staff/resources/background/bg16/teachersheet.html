Chronological Table


 

 

The prehistory and history of Mesopotamia is divided by archaeologists and historians into periods.

Sumer

  • 3500 - 3000  Uruk Period
  • 3000 - 2350  Early Dynastic
  • 2350 - 2100  Agade Empire
  • 2100 - 2000  Third Dynasty of Ur (or Ur III)

Babylonia

  • 2000 - 1600  Old Babylonian
  • 1600 - 1500  Dark Age
  • 1500 - 1150  Kassites
  • 1150 -   729  Various Dynasties
  •   729 -   625  Neo-Assyrian
  •   625 -   539  Neo-Babylonian
  •   539 -   331  Achaemenid Persian

Assyria

  • 2000 - 1600  Old Assyrian
  • 1600 - 1500  Dark Age
  • 1500 - 1350  Mitannian Empire
  • 1350 - 1000  Middle Assyrian
  • 1000 -   612  Neo-Assyrian
  •   612 -   539  Neo-Babylonian
  •   539 -   331  Achaemenid Persian
