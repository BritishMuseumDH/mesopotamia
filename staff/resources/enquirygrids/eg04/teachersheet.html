Diary of events

 

   Astronomy/Geography

 

Ask the pupils to complete the grid over seven days. At the same time and place each evening they should make a note of what they see in the sky (stars, sun, moon, weather conditions). At the end of the seven days they should write up their observations as a summary.

 

Complete a wall chart of the different types of observations. Discuss which events may be repeated at the same time next year. Which events can be foretold?
