How were baked bricks made?

Clay was pressed into moulds. When the clay was dry, the bricks were turned out. They were stacked together with gaps between them.


Brick making today.

The stack was covered with a dome of mud and then dry twigs were pushed through a hole in the side. The twigs were set on fire and allowed to burn for a long time. When cool, the bricks were removed from the dome. Firing the bricks made them water resistant.
