Often the only way an archaeologist can identify an ancient site or building is by reading the stamped bricks.

If you discovered this brick in the wall of a building you could find out the name of the city, what sort of building it was, which god lived there and which king had the building made.


Brick of Ur-Nammu

'For his lady Inanna, Ur-Nammu, the mighty man, the king of Ur, the king of Sumer and Akkad, built her temple.'
