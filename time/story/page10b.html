Modern countries are not always on good terms with each other, and when this happens archaeologists may not be able to work in them for a while. Areas may be restricted (for example, sites may be located in areas used by the army).

Archaeologists were unable to work in Iraq after the Gulf War of 1991, so many decided to investigate unexplored sites in Syria and Turkey instead.
